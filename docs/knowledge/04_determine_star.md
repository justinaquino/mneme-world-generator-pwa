## Stellar Classification Process

Roll **5D6 twice**:
1. First roll → stellar class (O/B/A/F/G/K/M)
2. Second roll → grade (0–9)

Establishes mass and luminosity for the star.

---

## Stellar Properties by Class

| Class | Color | Max Mass (M☉) |
|-------|-------|--------------|
| O | Blue | up to 128 |
| B | Blue white | up to 16 |
| A | White | up to 2.1 |
| F | Yellow white | up to 1.4 |
| G | Yellow | up to 1.04 |
| K | Light orange | up to 0.8 |
| M | Orange red | up to 0.45 |

---

## Companion Stars

Roll on Companion Star Table. A result of 12 allows rolling again (multi-star systems possible).

**Rule:** Companion stars must be equal or smaller in class and luminosity than the primary star.

### Orbital Distances

Roll 3D6 for companion star distance using an Orbit Table scaled by primary star class. Ranges from <0.125 AU to >50,000 AU.

---

## Habitable Zones (based on √Luminosity)

| Zone | Distance |
|------|----------|
| Infernal Zone | 0 to √L☉ × 0.4 AU |
| Hot Zone | √L☉ × 0.4 to √L☉ × 0.8 AU |
| Conservative Habitable Zone | √L☉ × 0.8 to √L☉ × 1.2 AU |
| Cold Zone | √L☉ × 1.2 to √L☉ × 4.85 AU |
| Outer Solar System | √L☉ × 4.85 AU onward |
