---

## Initial Roll

Roll **2D6** for world type on World Type Table A, then **2D6** for size.

**Star-type modifiers to world type roll:**
- F stars: Adv+2
- G stars: Adv+1

---

## World Types

### Habitats (Space Stations)

Population determined by life support capacity.
- Minimum TL: 9
- Population: 10,000 to billions depending on volume tons and TL

**Habitat Population Table:**

| TL | Volume Tons per Person |
|----|----------------------|
| TL7 | 100 VTons |
| TL10+ | 30 VTons |

### Dwarf Planets

Measured in Lunar masses.

**Lesser Earth Type Table (2D6):**
| Roll | Type | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| Most common | Carbonaceous | Carbon-rich |
| — | Silicaceous | Stony |
| Rare | Metallic | Dense |
| — | Other | — |

### Terrestrial Planets

"Made mostly of Silica or Rock; and heavier elements like iron; with mass measured in Earth Masses."

## Gravity

Roll on **DETERMINE GRAVITY Table A** (2D6).

| World Type | Gravity Range |
|------------|--------------|
| Dwarf planets | 0.001G – 1G |
| Terrestrial planets | 0.3G – 3G |
