# Chapter 7 — Determine Position

**Source:** https://wiki.gi7b.org/index.php/Mneme_World_Generator/Determine_Position
**Last Synced:** 2026-03-04

---

## Two Steps

1. Check World Qualities (atmosphere density + temperature) against Terrestrial World Qualities and Position Table to find valid orbital zone(s).
2. Roll 1D6 and apply Main World Position Table formula for AU distance from star.

If multiple zones apply, roll 1D6 to select.

---

## Main World Position Table

| Zone | Distance Formula |
|------|-----------------|
| Infernal | √L☉ × (0.067 AU × 1D6) |
| Hot | √L☉ × ((0.067 AU × 1D6) + 0.4 AU) |
| Conservative Habitable | √L☉ × ((0.067 AU × 1D6) + 0.7 AU) |
| Cold | √L☉ × ((0.61 AU × 1D6) + 1.2 AU) |
| Outer Solar System | √L☉ × ((1D6)² + 4.85 AU) |
